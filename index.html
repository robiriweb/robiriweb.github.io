Why Join IRI?

üß† Cutting-Edge Research

The institute, founded in 1995, is a key player in the Spanish robotics and automatic control scenes, and a valued participant in a large number of international collaborations. The research of PERCEPTION AND MANIPULATION group focuses on enhancing the perception, learning, and planning capabilities of robots to achieve higher degrees of autonomy and user-friendliness during everyday manipulation tasks.

üåç Global Collaborations

IRI plays a pivotal role in both national and international robotics research. As a member of euRobotics, IRI collaborates with a diverse array of stakeholders to drive the European robotics agenda forward. Furthermore, its co-direction of the ELLIS Unit Barcelona underscores its dedication to excellence in machine learning and artificial intelligence research. IRI also contributes to the Confederation of Laboratories for Artificial Intelligence Research in Europe (CLAIRE), promoting synergy among AI research labs across the continent. Through these collaborations, IRI not only enhances its research capabilities but also plays a significant role in shaping the future of robotics and informatics on a global scale.
